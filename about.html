Welcome to our lab!



Our lab, led by Benchi Wang, mainly focus on the research regarding a wide array of subjects within the field of attention and memory. To explore these interesting research, we adopt a wide range of methods, including behavioral testing, eye-tracking, EEG-recording, functional MRI, and modeling.

For more information on the topics we investigate, please refer to our research page and recent publications.
