ABOUT AMLAB

Our lab focuses primarily on the intricate domains of human attention, memory, learning, and language. Employing a diverse array of cognitive and neural techniques, including but not limited to eye-tracking, scalp and intracranial EEG, fMRI, and modeling, we delve into various facets of these topics. Our overarching goal is to unveil the fundamental mechanisms that underpin basic cognitive abilities. As an open and collaborative team, we enthusiastically welcome partnerships and collaborations from around the world.
