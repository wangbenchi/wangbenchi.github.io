AM-LAB Joins the Greater Bay Area Brain Science Center at Southern Medical University

AM-LAB进驻南方医科大学粤港澳大湾区脑科学与类脑研究中心

March 1, 2026

Starting March 1, 2026, AM-LAB (Attention and Memory Laboratory) will continue its research at the Greater Bay Area Center for Brain Science and Brain-Inspired Intelligence at Southern Medical University.

AM-LAB investigates the neural mechanisms of attention and memory, combining behavioral experiments, EEG, fMRI, and human intracranial electrophysiology (iEEG) to understand how the brain selects, encodes, and stores information. Research from the lab has appeared in journals including Nature Human Behaviour, PNAS, Communications Biology, and Trends in Cognitive Sciences.

The new center provides a multidisciplinary platform integrating neuroscience, medicine, and engineering, together with extensive clinical resources from Southern Medical University’s affiliated hospital network. This environment will facilitate new collaborations and support the lab’s research on brain dynamics, cognition, and neural computation.

By joining this center, AM-LAB aims to further advance fundamental research in cognitive neuroscience and strengthen collaborations across the Greater Bay Area and international research communities.

自 2026年3月1日 起，AM-LAB（Attention and Memory Laboratory） 将在南方医科大学粤港澳大湾区脑科学与类脑研究中心开展后续科研工作。

AM-LAB长期致力于研究注意与记忆的神经机制，结合行为实验、脑电、功能磁共振以及人类颅内电生理（iEEG）等方法，探索大脑如何选择、编码并存储信息。团队相关研究成果已发表于 Nature Human Behaviour、PNAS、Communications Biology、Trends in Cognitive Sciences 等国际期刊。

粤港澳大湾区脑科学与类脑研究中心整合神经科学、医学与工程学等多学科资源，并依托南方医科大学附属医院体系的临床平台，为脑科学研究提供重要支撑。

依托这一新平台，AM-LAB将进一步推动认知神经科学的基础研究，并加强与粤港澳大湾区及国际科研团队的合作。
