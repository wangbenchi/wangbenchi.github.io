AM-lab achieves great success at SS forum 2025

From July 14th to 17th, 2025, our group participate in the Third Scholar Forum on Quantitative Research Methods in Social Sciences at Dali University. At the forum, lab members Peiyao Xi and Haoyu Chen presented their respective research findings and progress in the sub-forums Neuroscience Indicators and Applications and Empowering Psychology and Education Research with Intelligent Technologies (Large Language Models).

Academic Highlights

During the forum, our team took part in many academic activities. They shared their research ideas with scholars and had discussions about how to design experiments, analyze data, and understand theories. These conversations helped us learn new things and inspired fresh ideas for their current and future research. Taking part in the forum not only gave the team valuable academic experience but also made us well-known in other fields. We continue to focus on doing careful, high-quality research and working with others across different fields, aiming to make new discoveries in cognitive and behavioral science.
